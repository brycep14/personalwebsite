About Me

I'm a student currently in BYU's Advertising Program with a knack for creativity and new ideas.

I love bringing artistic and interesting ideas to life and adding my own personal touch to the world around me.

I freaking love...

...all sorts of things.

Currently, some of the things I freaking love the most are basketball and art

My urge to create art has turned into a joke among our friends after a trip to thrift store ended in a old painting that we refurbished mosaic-style using paint samples acquired from Home Depot.

Balling up with friends is a must every Thursday, and don't get me started on the Oklahoma City Thunder. (Gosh I love this years team)

I'm always down for...

...all sorts of adventures. From epic car rides to Yellowstone or Arches, to abandoned mineshafts filled with grafitti, or just a trip downtown I'm always down for any and every kind of adventure, especially late night drives to cool places with music bumping in the background

Speaking of music...

...as the picture proves I'm basically an a-list celebrity at this point

I dabble in a few instruments and have performed the guitar in places as prestigious as my Grandma's background, a patio fireplace, and a church talent show. Superbowl Halftime Show, here I come.

I tend to have an earbud in any chance I can get away with it, and if you listen closely you can hear me belting it out to songs in the car and the shower. Horribly off-key unfortunately, but such is life. I'll listen to just about anything and everything so throw your music suggestions at me.

One of my biggest inspirations...

...for all my creative endeavors is Casey Niestat. The first time I watched this video, Do What You Can't, I was captivated and fell in love with his creativity, content style, and the way he always put his personal touch on his videos, belongings and world around him.
